Version 1.0.0
Objective: write a C program that will replicate a generic old-school RPG battle. This first version will feature you vs. a single enemy "Skeleton". You will have a choice of abilities: FIGHT, MAGIC, DEFEND, FLEE.
Magic will feature a submenu containing the spells: FIRE, ICE, BOLT, back, wherein back will return to the previous menu.
You will have 20 health, and the Skeleton will strike at you with a basic FIGHT command that will deal between 2-5 base damage. You will have a defense stat that will randomly negate between 1-3 damage. In addition, you will have a dodge stat that will have a 20% chance of taking zero damage.
